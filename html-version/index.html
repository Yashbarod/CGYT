Who We Are

More Than a Centre.
A Second Chance.

Founded in 2012, Nav Chetna was born from a simple belief: that every person struggling with addiction deserves dignity, expert care, and a genuine path to recovery.

We don't just treat addiction — we treat people. Our holistic approach combines medical detox, psychological counseling, yoga & meditation, vocational therapy, and family reintegration to rebuild whole, fulfilled lives.

Compassion First

We meet every individual without judgment — with warmth, dignity, and unconditional care.

Evidence-Based

Our treatment protocols are rooted in modern addiction science and validated clinical approaches.

Family Included

Recovery is a family journey. We counsel and support the entire support system, not just the patient.

Lifelong Support

Our commitment doesn't end at discharge. Aftercare programs and alumni networks keep you supported.
